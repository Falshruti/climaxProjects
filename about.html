About Us

We Craft Insight-Led, Data-Backed Campaigns That Turn Brand Stories Into Lasting Consumer Relationships


For over three decades, Climax Advertising has been the creative heartbeat of Kolhapur, shaping brand stories that don’t just land, but linger in minds. We began when hoardings were hand-painted and marketing meant more than metrics. Today, we build campaigns that travel from print to pixel, from katta conversations to smartphone scrolls.


Across Maharashtra, Goa, and Karnataka, brands trust us to blend local insight with creative impact, whether it’s a jingle that sticks in your head or a digital strategy that moves the needle.

Our Vision

To be a trusted creative partner for brands across industries and geographies. aim to deliver impactful advertising solutions that are grounded in cultural understanding, powered by strategy, and shaped to stand out in a fast-changing world.

Uday Joshi
Founder & Director, Climax Advertising

Still reads briefs with a pencil in hand. Still gets excited by a great headline.

A creative veteran with over three decades of experience, Uday Joshi is the force behind Climax Advertising. His journey began in Valpoi, Goa, and was shaped at the Sir J. J. School of Applied Arts, Mumbai. After cutting his teeth at a national agency in Mumbai, he returned with a vision to build something bold and lasting, right from Kolhapur. Since 1995, Uday has led Climax with sharp strategy, a client-first mindset, and a deep understanding of both media and market. From print campaigns to digital pivots, he’s seen the industry evolve and evolved with it. He doesn’t believe in shortcuts, but he does believe in smart budgets, strong ideas, and campaigns that stick. And while trends may come and go, Uday’s approach remains the same: clear, consistent, and deeply connected to the client’s goal.

Sundeep Desai
GM – Operations, Climax Advertising

Rarely misses a deadline. Almost never raises his voice. Definitely knows where every file is.

With over 30 years in media, production, and advertising, Sundeep Desai is the guy who keeps Climax running like clockwork, minus the drama. From Mumbai’s Bollywood sets to Pune’s newspaper houses, he’s handled everything from ad campaigns to AVs, packaging to print deadlines. At Climax since 2008, he’s been the go-to person for business development, media planning, troubleshooting, and branch operations. He’s the calm in the creative storm, the one who turns chaos into clarity, and timelines into reality.

Mangesh Joshi
Owner, Climax Digital

Likes his campaigns like his paintings — layered, balanced, and full of life.

An architect by education, a marketer by instinct, and an artist by heart, Mangesh Joshi brings the perfect blend of structure and creativity to Climax Digital. After completing his B.Arch from Pune University and gaining agency experience across categories like FMCG, entertainment, and fashion, he shifted gears into advertising full-time. His eye for detail is sharp, his problem-solving sharper. Mangesh believes leadership isn’t about titles, it’s about knowing the process, trusting the people, and pushing the work forward.

Rucha Joshi
Head of Digital, Climax Digital

Keeps her team on track, her copy tight, and her memes on point.

A commerce grad from BMCC Pune and a business management postgrad from Ontario, Canada; Rucha Joshi brings a global perspective and grounded instinct to every digital campaign. With a background in managing large-scale social and outdoor strategies for brands across real estate, jewellery, and education, she knows how to take a brand from quiet to scroll-stopping. At Climax Digital, she’s the driving force behind the agency’s fast-paced, high-performance culture. Clear-headed, people-first, and relentlessly focused on results, Rucha makes the digital game feel effortless.
